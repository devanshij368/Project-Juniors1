Routine

A skincare routine is important for keeping skin clean and healthy. A basci routine usually includes washing the face, applying moisturizer and using sunscreen. Doing these steps every day helps remove dirt, protect the skin and keep it balance

Many People prefer simple skincare routines because they are easy to follow. Using too many products can irritate the skinm so sticking to a few essential steps works better. A consistent routine helps improve skin texture and prevents common skin problems.
