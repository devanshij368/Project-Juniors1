Clean Makeup

Clean makeup is a beauty style that focuses on natural skin, minimal products, and a fresh appearance. It enhances facial features without covering the skin heavily.

This look often uses lightweight products such as tinted moisturizer, concealer, cream blush, mascara, and lip balm. These products help create a glowing and healthy finish.

Clean makeup is sometimes called the “no-makeup makeup” look because it appears effortless while still looking polished.

This style is popular for everyday wear because it feels comfortable, breathable, and easy to apply.
