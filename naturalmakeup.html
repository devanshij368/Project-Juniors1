Natural Makeup

Natural makeup focuses on enhancing a person's features while keeping the overall look soft and effortless. This style is meant to look simple, fresh, and close to a person's natural appearance.

Unlike bold or glam makeup, natural makeup uses minimal products such as light foundation or concealer, neutral eyeshadow, mascara, and lip balm. These products help create a clean and balanced look.

Earthy and neutral tones like beige, soft browns, and light pinks are commonly used because they blend well with natural skin tones and do not stand out too much.

Natural makeup is popular for everyday wear because it feels comfortable, looks polished, and works well for school, work, and casual activities.
