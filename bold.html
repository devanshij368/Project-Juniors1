Bold

Bold makeup is all about making a strong statement. It includes dramatic colors, sharp eyeliner and intense lip shades. This style is often used to express creativity and confidence.

Bright eyeshadow, dark lipstick, or unique designs are common in bold makeup looks. Bold makeup allows people to show their personality and try new trends without limits.
