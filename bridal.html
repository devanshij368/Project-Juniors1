Bridal

Bridal makeup focuses on a soft, elegant and timeless look. It is designed to enhance natural beauty while lasting throughout the entire day. The makeup is usually light but long lasting to look good in person and in photos.

Neutral colors, smooth foundation, and subtle highlights are commonly used. Bridal makeup aims to create a fresh and graceful appearance without looking too heavy.
