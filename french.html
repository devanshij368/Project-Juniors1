French Nails

French nails are a timeless and elegant nail style that never goes out of fashion. They are known for their clean look, usually featuring a soft pink or nude base with white tips.

This nail style is popular because it looks neat, natural, and professional. French nails are perfect for school, work, weddings, and formal events because they match almost any outfit.

Modern French nails have evolved from the classic style. Today, people experiment with different tip colors, glitter edges, pastel tones, and even chrome finishes while still keeping the clean French shape.
