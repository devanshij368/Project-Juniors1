Glam Makeup is designed to create a polished and eye catching look. It usually includes full coverage foundation, defined eyes and well blended colors. Glam makeup is often worn for special occastions when people want to look confident and put together.

This style focuses on smooth skin, detailed eye makeup and finished lips. Products like highlighter, counter and setting spray are commonly used. Glam makeup helps enhance facial features and creates a professional finished appearance.
