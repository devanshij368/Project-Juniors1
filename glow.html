Glow

Glowing skin is a popular beauty trend that focuses on a fresh and healthy appearance. This look is achieved by keeping the skin hydrated and well cared for. When skin is healthy, it naturally looks smooth and radiant.

Many people aim for glowing skin by drinking enough water and using moisturizing products. The glow trend encourages a natural look and helps people feel confident without heavy makeup.
