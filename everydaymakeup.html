Everyday Makeup

Everyday makeup focuses on natural beauty and simple techniques that enhance your features without looking heavy. These looks are perfect for school, work, or casual outings because they are quick and comfortable.

Popular everyday makeup includes light foundation or tinted moisturizer, mascara, lip gloss, and soft blush. The goal is to look fresh and confident while keeping the routine easy and stress-free.

Many people prefer neutral colors such as browns, pinks, and peaches because they work well with all skin tones and outfits.
